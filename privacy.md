Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Blade Lab handles your personal information. Your data belongs to you, and we take your privacy seriously.

By using this app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Data We Collect

Our app collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

• Player profile information (name, age, playing level)
• Workout history and training logs
• Padel activity sessions and match records
• Exercise performance data and progress metrics
• Assessment results and physical measurements
• App preferences and settings

All data is collected solely to provide you with personalized training programs and track your athletic progress.

How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored using Apple's Core Data framework and remains exclusively on your device. If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your data may be synced to your personal iCloud account for backup and synchronization across your devices.

• All data is stored locally on your device
• Optional iCloud sync through your Apple account
• No data is transmitted to our servers
• We do not have access to your information

Your iCloud data is encrypted and protected by Apple's security measures. Only you can access this information through your Apple ID.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

• Analytics or usage statistics
• Location data
• Device identifiers for advertising
• Third-party cookies or tracking data
• Personal information beyond what you explicitly enter

We have no servers collecting your information. Your privacy is maintained by keeping everything local to your device.

Your Right to Delete Data

You have complete control over your data:

• Navigate to Settings > Advanced > Delete All Data to permanently remove all your information from the app
• Deleting the app from your device will also remove all locally stored data
• If you have iCloud sync enabled, you may need to delete data from iCloud separately through your device settings

Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at hello@bladelab.app.

We will respond to your inquiries as promptly as possible.
